    • Cute Food Illustrations

    Ever since I was a child, I always leaned toward doodling food. Now in college, I find myself I still creating food illustrations. I also lean to clean vector drawings with cute and friendly personalities.

    This was a personal side project of cute graphic illustrations of food and drinks that were part of my life. I experimented with different styles I was not used to, like gradients and textured brushes.

    The subjects were based on my real life experiences of what food and drinks I have had during the time I created them, including a movie I love, Joker. Style inspirations are from cartoons, anime, Japanese kawaii doodles and plushies.

    One of the illustrations were animated into a looping GIF. Some of them were also created and sold into stickers for my Graphic Design BFA class at San Jose State University.
