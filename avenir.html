    • Avenir Type Specimen Booklet

    Avenir is a sans-serif typeface that was created by Adrian Frutiger in 1987 for Linotype GmbH. Avenir is French for "future," which shows its modern and geometric style.

    This type specimen booklet highlights its history in the making, impact and its construction of Avenir.

    The black and white colors highlights its modern simplicity in its form and the red color represents the Linotype brand that it was created for. Transparency illustrates its delicacy as well. The type treatment also imitates geometric forms of straight lines and right angles.

    Promotional materials were designed as well, such as a tote bag and a website mockup.
