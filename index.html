    About Jaerica Vitug

    I am a graphic designer based in San Jose, CA and currently attending San Jose State University as a Graphic Design BFA student, class of 2021.

    I'm looking for an internship showing how UI/UX design and branding works in the real-world.

    I believe fries are necessary with any burger.

    I was freelancing for NexTech AR Solutions by redesigning a eCommerce website for their subsidiary brand, Infinite Pet Life. Since 2016, I've been designing graphics for Iglesia Ni Cristo in San Jose, CA. I design posters, social media ads, flyers, banners and other promotional items for gatherings.

    I was also featured in exhibitions, such as in the Beyond Borders exhibition presented at the International Design Education Expo & Conference in 2019. Other exhibitions in the past are Eat, Drink, Vote and Justice For All? from the Euphrat Museum at De Anza College, also dealing with social justice.

    In the past, I attended De Anza college and discovered graphic design. I am now at San Jose State University to graduate with a Graphic Design BFA in Spring 2021. Today, I strive to learn more how graphic design impacts the world in branding and in UI/UX design.
